Game Partnership & Coordinator Rules

üîπ General Expectations

  • Professional Conduct: Respectful, inclusive behavior is expected at all times.
  • Clear Communication: Maintain regular, open, and honest communication.
  • Confidentiality: Internal materials and unreleased content must not be shared without permission.
  • Fair Treatment: All members, regardless of skill level, age, or experience, must be treated equally and fairly.
  • Credit Sharing: Everyone who contributes meaningfully must be credited in the final product or project page.

üîπ Game Partnership Rules

  • Default Ownership: Geo Interactive will normally own the game unless otherwise agreed in writing before development begins.
  • Non-Commercial Requirement: Commercial games are not allowed. All partnered projects must be non-profit, passion-driven, and free to play unless granted specific written permission from Geo Interactive.
  • Credit & Branding: All partnered games must include the Geo Interactive logo and proper acknowledgements.
  • Project Status Reporting: Partners must provide regular updates (weekly or bi-weekly preferred).
  • Scope & Deadlines: Projects must have a clear scope and timeline. Delays must be communicated early.
  • Asset & Code Sharing: Geo Interactive may request access to builds, assets, or source files for internal use only.
  • Cross-Contamination: You may not assign co-owner or leadership roles in your game to owners of other active partner games without Geo Interactive approval.

üîπ Game Coordinator Rules

  • Team Leadership: Game Coordinators are responsible for managing their team and progress.
  • Reporting & Documentation: Submit weekly reports and keep clear documentation of progress and decisions.
  • Conflict Resolution: Address issues constructively; escalate to Geo Interactive if unresolved.
  • Representation: Coordinators are official Geo Interactive representatives and must act professionally.
  • Permission Requests: Large gameplay, visual, or story changes must be reviewed by Geo Interactive leadership.
  • Overseer Role Requirement: Geo or the assigned overseer must be given the @Overseer role in your Discord server for access and transparency.
  • No Cross-Ownership: You cannot make another active game's owner a co-owner or lead in your project unless cleared by Geo Interactive.

üîπ Termination & Breach

  • Breaking these rules may result in removal from the partnership, loss of game support, or project suspension.
  • Warnings will usually be given unless a serious violation occurs.
