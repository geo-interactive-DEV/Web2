Last Updated: June 3, 2025

Introduction

Geo Interactive ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Services. By using our Services, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Including your name, email, date of birth (for age verification), and any other information you provide.
  • Usage Data: Data on how you use our Services such as IP address, browser type, pages visited.
  • Communications: Any messages, reports, or content you send to us.

2. Use of Your Information

  • To verify your age and ensure compliance with our no-under-13 policy.
  • To provide, operate, and improve our Services.

3. Age Verification

Users under 13 are not permitted to use our Services. We may request your date of birth to verify your age. This data is used only temporarily and not stored permanently. Users under 13 will be banned or denied access.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share information with trusted third parties who assist us under confidentiality agreements.

Any work or content you submit to Geo Interactive is owned by Geo Interactive, as stated in our Terms of Service.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed for service provision or legal requirements. Age verification data is deleted after confirming your age.

8. Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • You may opt out of receiving certain communications.
  • Contact us anytime at contact@geointeractive.online for privacy concerns.

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use means you accept the changes.
